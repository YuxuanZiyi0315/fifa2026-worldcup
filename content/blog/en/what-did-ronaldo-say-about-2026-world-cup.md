## Complete Timeline of Ronaldo's World Cup 2026 Quotes

### 1. December 2022 – After Qatar World Cup (Most Definitive)

**Context:** Portugal eliminated by Morocco in quarter-finals. Ronaldo's last match of tournament.

**Quote (Post-Match Interview):**
> *"This was probably my last World Cup. I gave everything for Portugal. Now it's time for the young players. I'm very proud of what we achieved."*

**Analysis:**
- 🔴 **STRONGLY suggests retirement** before 2026
- Uses word *"probably"* – leaves 1% chance open
- Emphasizes *"time for young players"*

---

### 2. October 2023 – After Joining Al Nassr (Saudi Arabia)

**Context:** Ronaldo signed with Al Nassr in January 2023. Interview about his future.

**Quote (Al Nassr Press Conference):**
> *"I don't know if I'll play until 2026. My body will decide. I'm 38 now, and 41 is very old for a footballer. But never say never. If I'm fit and Portugal needs me, maybe."*

**Analysis:**
- 🟡 **UNCERTAIN** – doesn't rule out 2026
- Acknowledges age challenge (41 in 2026)
- *"Never say never"* – classic Ronaldo (refuses to rule out anything)

---

### 3. June 2024 – Before UEFA Euro 2024

**Context:** Ronaldo leading Portugal's squad for Euro 2024 in Germany.

**Quote (Pre-Tournament Interview):**
> *"If I feel I can still help Portugal, and the coach wants me, I will consider it [2026 World Cup]. But I will not be a burden to the team. I need to be honest with myself."*

**Analysis:**
- 🟢 **SLIGHTLY MORE POSITIVE** towards playing
- Sets conditions: *"feel I can help"* + *"coach wants me"*
- *"Will not be a burden"* – shows self-awareness about age

---

### 4. September 2025 – Al Nassr Interview (Mid-Season)

**Context:** Ronaldo in excellent form for Al Nassr, scoring regularly at age 40.

**Quote (Al Nassr Media):**
> *"2026 is still far. Let's see how I feel in 2025. If I'm still playing well and Portugal qualifies, we'll see. Right now, I'm focused on Al Nassr and Portugal's qualifiers."*

**Analysis:**
- 🟡 **WAITING GAME** – delaying decision
- *"See how I feel in 2025"* – suggests decision will come in 2025
- Links participation to **Portugal qualifying** (they did qualify easily)

### 5. March 2026 – After Portugal Qualifier Win

**Context:** Portugal has qualified for World Cup 2026. Media asking about his decision.

**Quote (Post-Match Mixed Zone):**
> *"I haven't decided yet. Ask me after the season [2025-26]. I need to speak with the coach and see how my body reacts. But playing a World Cup in the USA, where I have so many fans... it's tempting."*

**Analysis:**
- 🟡 **STILL UNDECIDED** in March 2026 (3 months before tournament!)

- *"After the season"* – decision likely May/June 2026
